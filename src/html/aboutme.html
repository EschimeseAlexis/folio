About Me (Hover a text to Zoom-in)

I was born in Monaco in 2000. I had touched my first computer at the age of 3 and that's when passion hit me hard. I never gave up since. It evolved, like all teenagers, I spent days and nights playing video games with friends.. But one day, all suddenly I couldn't bear playing games anymore, so I slowly became attracted to programming and since that day I never stopped. I was 15 if I can remember well and made my way as an autodidact learning on multiple sites like OpenClassRoom or Codecademy.. Later I went to university to finally study Computer sciences and managed my way to Polytech Nice, an engineering school where I can happily fulfill my thirst of knowledge.

I particularly like Web-programming. In fact I really like design and aestheticism, and the way front-end development links those with programming is the reason why I like it this much. I started not so long ago. I started with the basics and I know that I have still a long path to go, but I'm determined, I'm motivated and I will succeed ! I'm actually looking for work-study contract and especially in front-end / full-stack web development, I'm ready to learn about all technos and frameworks that exists so if my profile has caught your attention, do not hesitate to contact me !
